What is FeeDings?
FeeDings is an RSS reader that puts you back in control of your content. No algorithms deciding what you see, no infinite scroll designed to keep you hooked, no timeline you did not ask for. You subscribe to what you want and read it how you want.
RSS is not dead. It is freedom from algorithmic feeds.
Features
Scroll or Flip - Choose your reading style. Scroll through feeds like a timeline, or flip pages like a book. Enable auto-scroll or auto-flip for hands-free reading.
Read in App - Full article reader built in. No need to leave the app. Read everything in your customised, distraction-free environment with your chosen theme and layout.
Ding! Your Favourites - Never miss what matters. Ding your favourite feeds to get priority notifications and keep them at the top.
ShareDing! - Share articles with friends directly through the app. The Add to Feedings button lets anyone subscribe to a feed you share with one tap.
News, Podcasts, Reddit, YouTube - Not just traditional RSS. Subscribe to Reddit feeds, YouTube channels, podcast feeds, and standard news sources, all in one unified reader.
Consent-Based Ads - FeeDings is supported by consent-based advertising. Users in the EEA/UK and applicable US states are presented with privacy choices before seeing personalised ads. No tracking without your permission.
Privacy
FeeDings respects your privacy. Ad personalisation requires your explicit consent. Your reading habits, feed subscriptions, and favourites stay on your device.
